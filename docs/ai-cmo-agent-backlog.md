# Trusted Bums AI CMO Agent Backlog

_Created: 2026-06-17 by Codex CEO/CMO setup._

## Executive Read

Trusted Bums should hire an AI Chief Marketing Officer Agent now as the `Growth` ELT doer for basic marketing strategy, public credibility, buyer-led positioning, and early demand creation. This is not a replacement for a human founder, fractional CMO, or public-channel owner. It is the internal operating agent that turns the current proof-safe story into approved marketing building blocks and keeps gaps visible.

The first problem is not broad paid marketing. The first problem is credibility and clarity. A serious buyer who checks Trusted Bums should quickly understand what the company does, why relationship-led access is different from cold lead generation, what claims are safe, and what the next step is. The public LinkedIn company page is a useful first surface because it currently looks underfilled from the logged-out public view and because B2B buyers commonly use LinkedIn as a verification stop.

- Stop condition: The basics are filled out, source-backed, approved, measured, and owned by a recurring human or hybrid Growth owner.
