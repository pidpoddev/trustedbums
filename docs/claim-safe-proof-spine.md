# Trusted Bums Claim-Safe Proof Spine

_Created: 2026-06-09 by Codex for TB-0035._

## Purpose

This is the reusable proof source for founder-led sales, referral asks, one-pagers, LinkedIn posts, marketing graphics, Content, B2B Growth, Trust, and Bum recruiting. Use this document before scaling demand, publishing proof claims, writing founder scripts, or asking agents to create campaign assets.

The goal is a serious, claim-safe story: Trusted Bums helps companies pursue hard-to-reach accounts through reviewed, relationship-led access workflows. The story must not imply guaranteed meetings, guaranteed revenue, guaranteed buyer interest, unapproved customer relationships, or guaranteed Bum payouts.

## Core Proof Spine

### One-Line Story

Trusted Bums helps companies work the accounts they cannot reach cold by routing serious Client demand through reviewed, relationship-led introduction workflows.

### Main Offer

One credible route can change the odds on a company-defining account.

### Source-Backed Narrative

Important buyers often ignore strangers. Trusted Bums exists because strategic-account access is usually a trust problem before it is a messaging problem. Clients bring specific accounts or buyer types they cannot reach through normal outbound. Reviewed Bums bring relationship context, warm-access potential, and commercial judgment. Trusted Bums turns those pieces into a controlled workflow for intake, qualification, introduction support, follow-up, reporting, commissions, payouts, and auditability.

The brand can stay playful because the process is serious. The public story should make that contrast clear: the name is memorable, but the motion is selective, reviewed, and designed for high-trust commercial access.

## Approved Public Proof Categories

These claims can be used publicly when phrased conservatively and tied to the current product or operating model.

- Trusted Bums is a managed B2B relationship marketplace for warm-introduction and access workflows.
- Clients bring named-account or hard-account demand that cold outreach has not solved.
- Approved Bums contribute relationship context, credibility, and potential access paths.
- The workflow supports Client intake, target-account context, Bum review, opportunity claims, meeting support, reporting, commission review, payout handling, and audit trails.
- Trusted Bums aligns economics with successful commercial outcomes when approved relationship support contributes to collected Client revenue, subject to agreement terms and opportunity rules.
- Trusted Bums uses review and policy boundaries to reduce misleading relationship claims and unsupported public proof.
- Trusted Bums does not guarantee meetings, introductions, customer responses, contracts, revenue, approvals, deal timing, payout approval, or continued platform access.

## Private Or Internal-Only Proof

These items can guide founder calls, manual qualification, support, and internal agent analysis, but they must not be published or reused in public collateral unless explicitly approved.

- Client names, target accounts, buyer names, opportunity details, CRM notes, meeting notes, transcripts, call summaries, extension-derived data, internal research, and private relationship maps.
- Customer logos, testimonials, case studies, screenshots, quotes, before-and-after outcomes, revenue amounts, payout amounts, and commission examples without written permission.
- Founder-call objections, disqualification reasons, source attribution, referral-source identity, investor/advisor names, and internal prospect quality scoring.
- Legal, compliance, finance, and payout review details beyond already-published policy summaries.
- Any evidence collected under a private Client, Bum, advisor, investor, or partner relationship.

## Do Not Say

The following claims are forbidden unless Legal and the accountable business owner approve a narrow replacement in writing.

- "We guarantee meetings."
- "We guarantee introductions."
- "We guarantee revenue."
- "We can get you into any account."
- "Our Bums already know your target buyer."
- "Trusted Bums has verified every relationship as true."
- "You will get paid for joining."
- "Bums earn passive income."
- "We already work with [customer logo]" without written logo or reference permission.
- "Trusted Bums replaces your sales team."
- "Trusted Bums is an appointment-setting agency."
- "Trusted Bums scrapes private relationship data."
- "Trusted Bums is certified for [security/compliance standard]" unless the certification is current and documented.
- Any claim that a Bum is an employee, agent, broker-dealer, financial advisor, procurement representative, or authorized buyer representative unless separately documented.

## Variant: Client Acquisition

### Use When

Founder-led sales, Client one-pagers, Client landing-page follow-up, referral traffic, and qualified strategy-review calls.

### Message

Bring us the accounts your team cannot crack. Trusted Bums helps you evaluate whether a credible warm route exists, then works the process inside reviewed relationship and commercial boundaries.

### Proof Points To Use

- The marketplace is built around hard-account demand, not generic lead volume.
- The Client intake path asks for buyer role, account context, urgency, blocker, referral source, and commercial reason.
- Admin review separates qualified strategy requests from general inquiries before escalation.
- The workflow is designed for controlled access, not spray-and-pray outbound.
- Trusted Bums does not promise a meeting or outcome; it promises a serious process for testing whether a credible route exists.

### CTA

Request a strategy review for the specific accounts your team cannot reach cold.

## Variant: Investor Or Advisor Referral Ask

### Use When

Asking investors, advisors, operators, and trusted commercial connectors for warm referrals to qualified Client prospects.

### Message

Who do you know with a short list of accounts that matter and no credible way in? Trusted Bums is looking for companies with real named-account urgency, not broad lead-list demand.

### Proof Points To Use

- The right referral is a company with strategic-account urgency and a clear internal owner.
- Trusted Bums screens for seriousness before treating demand as active marketplace liquidity.
- Referral sources do not need to promise access, outcomes, or insider influence.
- Any compensation, disclosure, or endorsement language must stay out of the ask unless Legal approves it.

### CTA

Introduce one founder, CRO, VP Sales, or GTM owner who has a specific blocked account and would value a selective strategy review.

## Variant: Bum Recruiting

### Use When

Invite-only recruiting, founder referral asks, operator outreach, and Bum onboarding explanations.

### Message

Trusted Bums is looking for credible operators who can help serious companies reach buyers through truthful relationship context and reviewed access workflows.

### Proof Points To Use

- Bum participation is selective and reviewed.
- Bums should describe relationship strength, access, and influence truthfully.
- Opportunities, claims, approvals, compensation, and continued access are governed by platform rules.
- Joining, completing a profile, proposing an introduction, or claiming an opportunity does not guarantee approval, payment, payout timing, or future opportunities.

### CTA

Apply or accept an invite only if you can operate inside truthful relationship claims and reviewed commercial boundaries.
