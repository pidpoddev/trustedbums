## Inputs Used

Known company context:

- Trusted Bums is a high-trust B2B warm-introduction marketplace.
- Clients define target accounts, training, commission terms, opportunities, meetings, payment reports, and legal terms.
- Bums claim relevant relationship access, support warm introductions, manage contact/prospect context, and track earnings/payouts.
- The product is not appointment setting, a lead list, an SDR agency, or a generic referral marketplace.
