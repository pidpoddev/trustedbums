## Reusable Visual System

- Prefer human relationship, warm-introduction, trust network, blocked-inbox, revenue-loop, and buyer-access motifs over generic SaaS dashboard scenes.
- Lead with the existing Trusted Bums visual system: deep ink, warm cream, action orange, and supporting trust teal.
- Keep brand text, headlines, CTAs, legal phrases, and stats in editable overlays or approved vector assets rather than generated bitmap text.
- Reject fake UI labels, malformed signs, gibberish badges, distorted logos, misspelled `Trusted Bums`, and any pseudo-text that could be mistaken for real campaign copy.
