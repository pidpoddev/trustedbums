## Text Handling

- All three files are intentionally text-free in visible artwork.
- Visible headlines, CTAs, logo lockups, proof claims, and legal-sensitive wording should be added later as editable text layers.
- The only words inside the SVG files are accessibility metadata in `<title>` and `<desc>`, which do not render into the artwork.

## Visual QA Summary

- All three plates were source-authored in SVG to keep visible type out of the artwork and preserve editable overlays.
- Manual source inspection confirmed there are no `<text>` nodes, no pseudo-badges, no fake UI labels, and no embedded raster watermarks.
- Rendered PNG inspection is still required before campaign approval to confirm crop stability and verify that no abstract shape resolves into character-like artifacts.
