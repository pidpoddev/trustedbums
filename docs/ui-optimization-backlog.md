## Active Recommendations

### P1 - [TB-0060] Keep the mobile `Privacy choices` control out of live authenticated content
- Evidence: Exact-head `Visual UI Audit` `27896715845` uploaded authenticated screenshots before failing on protected-route settle timeouts, and those captures confirm the bug on current head `5af32ed`. In `mobile-chrome-admin-admin-dashboard.png`, the left-aligned chip still lands over the lower-left of the `Priority queues` card and its pill controls while the chat launcher stays on the opposite side; in `mobile-chrome-admin-admin-handoffs.png`, it still covers the search and filter zone; in `mobile-chrome-client_admin-client-register-opportunity.png`, it still intrudes into the lower-left edge of the `Estimated value` card zone; and in `mobile-chrome-bum-bum-dashboard.png`, it still sits in the active transition between the profile-completion card and the metric stack. Current source confirms why: [ConsentManager.tsx](/Users/macdaddy/CodexWork/TrustedBums/trustedbums/src/components/ConsentManager.tsx) still mounts the control as a fixed overlay on authenticated routes, while [AdminLayout.tsx](/Users/macdaddy/CodexWork/TrustedBums/trustedbums/src/layouts/AdminLayout.tsx) still keeps the chat launcher on the opposite side. Current platform guidance still supports safe-area-aware placement through [MDN `env()`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS/Reference/Values/env), and current accessibility guidance still frames this as a placement issue because [W3C Focus Not Obscured (Minimum)](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/Understanding/focus-not-obscured-minimum.html) expects persistent disclosures not to cover the active interaction point.
- Why it matters: The product is cleaner than the old lower-right stack, but the remaining left-edge overlay still makes authenticated mobile routes feel improvised because it covers live cards and control zones instead of using a reserved utility lane.
- Recommendation: Treat the privacy launcher as an authenticated-mobile utility surface, not a floating card overlay. Either move it into an intentional support or account surface, or reserve a dedicated bottom utility lane that clears cards, pill controls, filters, queue copy, and form fields across Admin, Client, and Bum shells.
- Acceptance criteria: Fresh exact-head mobile screenshots for `/admin`, `/client/opportunities/new`, and `/bum/dashboard` show the `Privacy choices` control outside live cards, tabs, pill controls, filters, and form surfaces. If Admin still keeps the chat launcher, both utilities clear content simultaneously. The final placement remains reachable and uses `env(safe-area-inset-bottom)`.
