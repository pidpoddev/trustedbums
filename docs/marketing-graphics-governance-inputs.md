# Trusted Bums Marketing Graphics Governance Inputs

_Created: 2026-06-18 by Codex low-risk scrum pass._

## Purpose

This file is the input checklist for `TB-0080`. It does not approve new claims, assets, spend, or publishing. It records the non-secret decisions and evidence Marketing Graphics needs before scaling campaigns beyond the current proof-safe asset pack.

Use this checklist before creating text-bearing ads, customer-proof graphics, founder-led campaign visuals, paid placements, or new brand templates.

## Current Operating Rule

The active creative system stays source-backed and conservative:

- Use the current text-free 2026-06-09 asset pack for campaign assembly.
- Keep Trusted Bums text, headlines, CTAs, stats, proof points, and legal-sensitive copy in editable layers.
- Do not imply guaranteed meetings, introductions, revenue, buyer responses, payouts, or customer relationships.
- Do not use customer logos, testimonials, personal names, screenshots, or relationship claims without explicit approval.
- Do not create fake dashboards, fake buttons, fake notifications, pseudo-text, or UI-like ad controls.
