# Trusted Bums Brand Strategy

_Created: 2026-06-20 by Codex from Ryan-approved direction and the claim-safe proof spine._

## Positioning

Trusted Bums is a selective B2B relationship-access marketplace for companies trying to work important accounts they cannot reach cold.

The brand should feel serious, plain-spoken, selective, and trust-centered. The name can be memorable and informal because the operating model is reviewed, controlled, and explicit about what it does not promise.

## Audience Priority

1. Client buyers with one or more named accounts that matter and no credible current route in.
2. Investors, advisors, operators, founders, and senior sellers who can refer qualified Client demand.
3. Approved or prospective Bums who have truthful relationship context and can operate inside reviewed rules.
4. Owner, legal, finance, trust, and operations stakeholders who need proof that the marketplace is controlled.

## Message Spine

- Important buyers often ignore strangers.
- Trusted access can change whether a serious account conversation is possible.
- Clients bring specific hard-account demand, not generic lead-list requests.
- Reviewed Bums contribute relationship context, credibility, and possible warm-access paths.
- Trusted Bums turns those pieces into a controlled workflow for intake, qualification, opportunity support, follow-up, reporting, commission review, payout handling, and auditability.

## Primary CTA

Request a strategy review for the specific accounts your team cannot reach cold.

## Proof Boundaries

Use only claim-safe language unless Ryan or another named owner approves a stronger claim in writing.

Do not imply:

- guaranteed meetings
- guaranteed introductions
- guaranteed revenue
- guaranteed buyer response
- guaranteed payout
- verified access to every target buyer
- passive income for Bums
- customer names, logos, outcomes, or screenshots without permission

## Voice

- Direct and serious.
- Plain language over clever slogans.
- Selective rather than high-volume.
- Proof-aware rather than hype-driven.
- Confident about the process, careful about outcomes.

## Visual Direction

- Lead with real workflow credibility: strategy reviews, routed opportunities, relationship maps, review checkpoints, and owner-visible progress.
- Avoid generic handshake art, hype graphics, passive-income cues, cheap lead-gen imagery, and broad affiliate-style visuals.
- Keep generated artwork text-free unless editable overlay copy is reviewed against this strategy and `docs/claim-safe-proof-spine.md`.
- Preserve the current restrained, high-trust public-site feel unless a later owner-approved visual system replaces it.
